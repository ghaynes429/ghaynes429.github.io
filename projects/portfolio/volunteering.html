04Why this matters to me

Volunteering with kids has shaped how I think about people, patience, and community, the same things that draw me toward psychology.

Whether I'm running an art activity at MPSA or facilitating a community circle at Little Friends For Peace, I'm practicing the same skills: reading a room, adapting to what people need in the moment, and creating an environment where someone feels comfortable being themselves. Those are skills I want to carry into whatever career I build in psychology.
