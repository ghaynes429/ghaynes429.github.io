Strawberry Banana Smoothie?

Hello! I am Gabe and I'm gonna teach you how to make a very good strawberry banana smoothie!

Ingredients/Materials Needed:

  • 1.5 Cups of mixed strawberries and bananas (freeze for better texture)
  • 1/2 Cup of greek yogurt (Vanilla)
  • 1/3 Cup of 2% milk
  • (Not Required) 1 tbsp of strawberry syrup for sweetness
  • Crushed ice, about the size of a marble and smaller
