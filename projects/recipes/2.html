Banana Bread?

Hello! I am Gabe AGAIN and I'm gonna teach YOU how to make some superb banana bread!!! (Serves like 12-24 people)

This recipe is close to my heart as my mom has been making it since I was a wee lad.

I perfected it with help from your friend's mom

- Kia Stevenson-Haynes

Ingredients/Materials Needed

Ingredients Amount Needed
Bananas 3 1/2 Whole Bananas
All-Purpose Flour 1 2/3 Cups
Baking Soda 1 Tsp
Cinnamon 1/4 Tsp
Salt 1/2 Tsp
Sugar 1 Cup + 2 Tbsp
Large Eggs 2 Eggs
Oil (Olive) 1/2 Cup
Creme Fraiche 2 Tbsp
Walnuts (Toasted and Chopped) 2/3 Cup

Other Materials

  • 9x5 in. Pan
  • Multiple BIGGGG Bowls
  • Something to grease the pan
  • Oven

Step-by-Step

First, get your bowl and all your ingredients together

Procedure:

  1. Mash up bananas and put them in a bowl to refrigerate
  2. Put your flour, baking soda, cinnamon and salt in one bowl
  3. In another bowl, put your sugar and eggs and whisk them until light and fluffy (About 10 Minutes)
  4. Add your oil and mashed bananas into the bowl with the sugar and eggs and mix them in.
  5. Add the Creme Fraiche and Vanilla Extract into the same bowl, and mix
  6. Mix dry ingredients with the wet ingredients, and add your walnuts
  7. Mix all your ingredients until at the goopy consistency
  8. Grease your pan and put your mixture into the pan
  9. Put in pre-heated oven and bake for 350 degrees fahrenheit for 45-60 minutes
  10. Once it's finished baking, wait to cool, cut into even slices and serve!

Cool Tips and Tricks

  • Quadruple the amount of cinnamon
  • Double the amount of vanilla
  • Double the amount of creme fraiche
  • Lessen the amount of sugar to just a cup
  • Use 4 bananas instead of 3
