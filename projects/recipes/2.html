Banana Bread?

Hello! I am Gabe AGAIN and I'm gonna teach YOU how to make some superb banana bread!!! (Serves like 12-24 people)

This recipe is close to my heart as my mom has been making it since I was a wee lad.

I perfected it with help from your friend's mom

- Kia Stevenson-Haynes

Ingredients/Materials Needed

Ingredients Amount Needed
Bananas 3 1/2 Whole Bananas
All-Purpose Flour 1 2/3 Cups
Baking Soda 1 Tsp
Cinnamon 1/4 Tsp
Salt 1/2 Tsp
Sugar 1 Cup + 2 Tbsp
Large Eggs 2 Eggs
Oil (Olive) 1/2 Cup
Creme Fraiche 2 Tbsp
Walnuts (Toasted and Chopped) 2/3 Cup
